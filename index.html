Automated TikTok Posting—Made Easy

Personal Uploader is a lightweight .NET worker that uploads videos to your TikTok account through the official Content Posting API. It’s designed for solo-creators who want a clean CI/CD-style workflow: render → push → TikTok.

How it works

  1. Render or export a video file.
  2. Commit & push to GitHub (or drop into S3).
  3. Personal Uploader detects the new asset and calls TikTok’s API.
  4. The video appears in TikTok as Private (unless your app is fully audited).

Need details? See TikTok docs.
